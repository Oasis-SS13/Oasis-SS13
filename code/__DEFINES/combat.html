His Grace.

HIS_GRACE_SATIATEDHe hungers not. If bloodthirst is set to this, His Grace is asleep.
HIS_GRACE_PECKISHSlightly hungry.
HIS_GRACE_HUNGRYGetting closer. Increases damage up to a minimum of 20.
HIS_GRACE_FAMISHEDDangerous. Increases damage up to a minimum of 25 and cannot be dropped.
HIS_GRACE_STARVINGIncredibly close to breaking loose. Increases damage up to a minimum of 30.
HIS_GRACE_CONSUME_OWNERHis Grace consumes His owner at this point and becomes aggressive.
HIS_GRACE_FALL_ASLEEPIf it reaches this point, He falls asleep and resets.
HIS_GRACE_FORCE_BONUSHow much force is gained per kill.

bullet_act() return values

BULLET_ACT_HITIt's a successful hit, whatever that means in the context of the thing it's hitting.
BULLET_ACT_BLOCKIt's a blocked hit, whatever that means in the context of the thing it's hitting.
BULLET_ACT_FORCE_PIERCEIt pierces through the object regardless of the bullet being piercing by default.
BULLET_ACT_TURFIt hit us but it should hit something on the same turf too. Usually used for turfs.
